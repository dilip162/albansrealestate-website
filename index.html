You aren’t just a number or sales target

You aren’t just a number or a sales target at David Chadwick St Albans. We appreciate that moving, and the reasons for moving, are probably the biggest and most important thing going on in your life at that moment.

Whether you are considering an immediate move or require advice to prepare for the future we will provide a property appraisal and valuation at a time to suit you.

This is free of charge and without obligation, and will include a general market update, a local activity report with comparable evidence, advice on preparing the property for marketing and agreeing a tailored marketing campaign and selling strategy.

Throughout the selling process we will provide you with updates on the marketing, online activity and feedback from viewings. We know that the quality of communication is paramount in order to provide you with data on which you can make decisions with confidence, particularly when it comes to receiving offers and agreeing a sale.

All offers will be carefully assessed and qualified; we will guide all parties through the sales progression process to ensure a smooth exchange of contracts, completion and moving day.
